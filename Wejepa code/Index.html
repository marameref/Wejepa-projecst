A Biography of Nigeria's #1 Celebrity Blogger

LINDA IFEOMA IKEJI is a writer, an entrepreneur and a successful blogger who has mastered the art of feeding people the kind of news they want to hear through Linda Ikeji’s Blog and she makes cool cash while at it. Many youths especially young ladies are inspired by her success story and believe they can achieve anything they put their heart to.

Her Biography

Linda Ikeji was born on the 19th of September 1980 to a Catholic parent from Nkwerre, a local government area in the Southeastern state of Imo State. She started writing fictional stories at a young age of 10. Her fascination about news and interviews was what endeared her into pursuing a degree in Mass Communication. Unfortunately, she was admitted to study English in the University of Lagos instead of Mass Communication. Linda Ikeji received an honorary doctorate degree on the 8th of August 2018 from the Trinity International University of Ambassadors in Georgia for her contribution to African media and business.

Let’s take a leap into how Linda Ikeji started.

The Early Struggles of a Blogging Icon

As an average Nigerian born into a humble background, Linda Ikeji also had her fair share of financial struggle while growing up. In a bid to assist herself through school and support her parents as well, Linda started working from age 17, from modelling and bartendering to waitressing and ushering. She did not allow her background put her back but rather saw it as an opportunity to explore and sharpen her talent which is paying off today.
After University, Linda Ikeji worked hard to break into journalism by launching her own media company, Blackdove Communications. Blackdove Communications is a modelling agency and events management outfit. She also started a magazine called FM&B which release its first issue in 2006. After struggling with both businesses, she decided to give up on them. For FM&F, she struggled to get advertiser on board and also discovered how time consuming and capital intensive the project was, only two more editions saw the light of the day. Concerning Blackdove, Linda Ikeji had this to say, “I gave up on the office (Black Dove Communications) in 2010 when I couldn’t afford the rent anymore and moved my business back home. About a year later, the blogging I started in 2006 changed my life.”

Her Journey into Blogging

In 2006 that Linda Ikeji started blogging as a hobby using blogger.com with her blog name as lindaikeji.blogspot.com. She started blogging when the internet was not as prominent as it is today and she made her posts then from a cybercafe. Linda Ikeji had no set goals for her blogging hobby and blogged for many years before making any money from it. She later obtained lindaikejisblog.com that is viral today. The media entrepreneur is now a celebrity blogger who is considered to be one of the most successful bloggers in the world.
